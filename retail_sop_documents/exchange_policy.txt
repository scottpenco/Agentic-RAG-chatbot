Standard Operating Procedure: Exchange Policy

Policy: Customers may exchange items within 30 days of purchase.
Conditions:
- Items must be in resellable condition with tags attached.
- Even exchanges are processed without additional fees.
- Exchanges for a higher-priced item require the customer to pay the difference.

Procedure:
1. Inspect the item and verify eligibility.
2. Locate the new item for exchange.
3. Process the exchange in the POS system.
4. Provide a new receipt to the customer.
