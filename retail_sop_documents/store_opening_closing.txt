Standard Operating Procedure: Store Opening & Closing Procedures

Opening:
1. Arrive 30 minutes before store opening.
2. Perform a walk-through and ensure cleanliness.
3. Turn on registers and check POS connectivity.
4. Unlock store doors at the designated time.

Closing:
1. Announce store closing 15 minutes before.
2. Secure all cash in the register drop safe.
3. Perform a final walkthrough and lock all doors.
