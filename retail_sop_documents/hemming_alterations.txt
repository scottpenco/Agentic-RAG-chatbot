Standard Operating Procedure: Hemming & Alterations

Policy: Complimentary hemming is available for select products.
Conditions:
- Customers must present the original receipt.
- Hemming is available for standard-length adjustments only.

Procedure:
1. Verify the product and receipt.
2. Pin the garment according to customer request.
3. Send the item to the alterations department.
4. Notify the customer upon completion.
