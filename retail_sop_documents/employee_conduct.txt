Standard Operating Procedure: Employee Conduct & Customer Service

Policy: Employees must maintain professionalism and provide excellent customer service.
Expectations:
- Greet customers within 10 seconds of entering the store.
- Maintain a positive and helpful attitude.
- Resolve complaints calmly and efficiently.

Procedure:
1. Greet customers and offer assistance.
2. Provide product recommendations as needed.
3. Handle complaints professionally and escalate if necessary.
4. Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
