Standard Operating Procedure: Returns & Refunds Policy

Policy: Customers may return unused items within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
Conditions:
- The item must be in its original packaging.
- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
- Clearance and final sale items are not eligible for returns.
- Returns without a receipt will be issued store credit.

Procedure:
1. Verify the item condition and receipt.
2. Process the return through the POS system.
3. If eligible, refund the amount or issue store credit.
4. Update inventory records.
